Personal Life

I was born in Kabul, Afghanistan on May 25, 1981. 3 short months later, my family and I became refugees and moved to several locations before settling down in Hessen, Germany. At the early age of 3, we moved to Oxen Hill, MD to live the american dream. I graduated Bowie High School in 1999 and University of Maryland, College Park with a Bachelors in Arts & Humanities in 2004. Shortly before I graduated college, my mother passed away from melinma. Luckily, I still have my older sister, younger brother, father and we have a stepmother now. As many foreign families, we have a lot of family members. I love my cousins and we stay close. I currently reside in Crofton, MD with no kids or husband and I love it.


Professional Career

Fresh out of college, I worked at Summit Marketing. A Promotional product marketing company in Silver Spring, MD. I worked on a team that managed programs and my account was GEICO. I did promotional product research, managed custom orders, managed the inventory and the reporting. After over 3 years I moved on to GEICO. My dream job at that time.

At GEICO, I worked on the event marketing team and was a jack of all trades. I preformed every function on the team, from managing the $22 Million budget to getting in that horrible gecko costume. The tasks that I loved most were the ones that allowed me to be creative. That lead to an interest in graphic design. I self taught myself and like to create designs for myself in my free time. After over 7 years, I was decided to move on from GEICO. I knew all there was to know and wasn't learning anything new and felt stagnant. My last day at GEICO was Feb 27, 2015.

Since GEICO, I have been working as a consultant at a new marketing company, Montage Marketing. I have been working on their social media, digital marketing and graphic design. However, I am just doing this work for fun and to learn some new skillsets.

What is next for me? Web Development!
